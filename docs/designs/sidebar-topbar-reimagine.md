# CEO Plan: Reimagine Sidebar & Top Bar
Generated by /plan-ceo-review on 2026-03-18
Branch: master | Mode: SCOPE EXPANSION
Repo: Ankurjain1121/taskflow

## Vision

### 10x Check
Transform the sidebar from a file-browser-style nav (739-line monolith with duplicate controls) into a **workspace operating system**. Active workspace context scopes everything. Top bar becomes a contextual command center. Sidebar collapses to a polished icon rail. Notifications become a first-class inbox.

### Platonic Ideal
Opening TaskFlow feels like opening your workspace cockpit. The sidebar *knows* your context вҖ” your workspace, your pinned projects, your unread items. The top bar shows exactly where you are with contextual actions. One search (Ctrl+K) does everything. Keyboard-navigable end to end. Linear's restraint meets Monday.com's visual warmth.

## Accepted Scope (added to this plan)
- Workspace switcher dropdown at sidebar top with active workspace state
- Contextual top bar: workspace breadcrumbs, single search, page actions, deduplicated controls
- Sidebar reorganized into 4 zones: WS header, primary nav (Home/My Work/Inbox), Projects, Footer
- Full-page Inbox (/inbox) with sidebar nav item and unread badge
- Project color dots (8-accent palette) + drag-to-reorder in sidebar
- Arrow-key keyboard navigation with ARIA tree roles
- Workspace Views section (Portfolio, Team, Archive) scoped to active workspace
- Icon rail collapsed mode (48px) with tooltips and spring animation
