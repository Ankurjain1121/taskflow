# Accounts Workspace — Task Entry Sheet

**Fields:** Task = `title` + `priority` (required). Subtask = `title` only.
**Priority:** `urgent` · `high` · `medium` · `low`

---

## (a) Final Accounts | Yearly | Rinkal | Start: 1 Mar | Due: 31 Mar

- **Final Accounts Book Finalization Points** [high]
  1. Unsecure Loan Interest Given or not
  2. TDS Match with all challan
  3. GST match with portal
  4. Creditors Ledger match with party confirmation ledger
  5. Credit Card statement match with 31.3 closing balance
  6. Electricity bill match with original bills
  7. Fixed Asset ledger match and depreciation entry
  8. Loan & Advance Ledger Match
  9. Debtors Ledger match with party confirmation ledger
  10. Whether the expense needs to be recorded in the books of accounts or not
  11. Bank Balance match with 31.3 closing balance
  12. Stock match with original stock
  13. Secure Loan Statement match with 31.3 closing statement
  14. Rent Amount match with rent agreement
  15. If there is TDS receivable, entry should be made after verifying in Form 26AS

## (b) GST | Rinkal

- **GSTR-1** [urgent] | Monthly | Start: 1st | Due: 5th | Govt deadline: 11th
  1. Collect data from software
  2. Verify from physical printouts
  3. Prepare data
  4. Send to CA *(unassigned)*

- **GSTR-2B** [high] | Monthly | Start: 14th | Due: 16th | Govt auto-generates 14th
  1. Download GSTR-2B from portal
  2. Reconcile with purchase records
  3. Entry of missing invoices if not found in books
  4. Final report to CA *(unassigned)*

- **GSTR-3B** [medium] | Monthly | Start: 10th | Due: 17th | Govt deadline: 20th
  1. Prepare liability summary
  2. Pay GST liability
  3. File GSTR-3B

- **TDS Payment** [urgent] | Monthly | Start: 1st | Due: 5th | Govt deadline: 7th
  1. Calculate TDS liability
  2. Make payment on NSDL/TIN
  3. Save challan/receipt

- **TDS Return** [high] | Quarterly (Jul/Oct/Jan/Apr) | Start: 1st | Due: 10th | Govt deadline: 15th
  1. Prepare list of deductees
  2. Verify
  3. Send to CA *(unassigned)*
